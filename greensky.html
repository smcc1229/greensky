A celebration of Greensky Bluegrass

Written with the guidance of Epicodus

I found greensky at a late night set at Horning's Hideout.At first I just casually liked them. I saw them play a free show at a local records store. They played for a whole hour! It blew me away and I was made a fan for life. They are a pure joy to see live. The band has a lot of fans. Us fans are called campers :) As in happy campers
